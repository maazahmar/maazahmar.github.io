ABOUT

DESIGN

Modern | Clean | Minimalistic

A designer focused in usability of digital products and believes in minimalistic designs. Maaz Ahmar is a Google certified User Experience (UX) Designer, studying Information Technology at Tribhuvan University and lives in Biratnagar, Nepal. Currently, he is freelancing and working on his own ideas.

He is a multidisciplinary designer with the love and passion for product design, graphic design, interface design and values modern, clean & minimalist designs. He uses creative thinking and great design to solve design problems, passionately follows lateral thinking principles and admires Sir Edward de Bono.

TECH
